mac and cheese

When it happened: (first time) Unknown

Where it happened: Home (India)

Mac and cheese is easy instant food that never fails to comfort you. The creaminess of it just leaves you on cloud 9 (unless you're not a fan of the butter and cheese obviously). When you don't know what to cook or when you feeling like whipping something up for dinner in less than 15 minutes, this is what my mom always did. She'd make a pot full of Mac and cheese for my sister and we'd take our own portions. We'd bring the pot to the TV room, eat and watch TV simultaneously. We wouldn't care if the pasta went cold because well, cold Mac and cheese has its own taste too.
Memory highlights:
I took this box of Mac and cheese when I moved for college and it used to sit in my cabinet for the longest time because you don't get it in India. We only got these boxes when we had people visit from the US; so as you can see, it was too precious. This box of pasta, however, is WAY too much for just one person so my friend and I would find the right time to eat the pasta. The first time we made it, it was absolute heaven. We put on the show Friends, took a fork each and dug straight into the pan and probably scraped it to the last bit. The second time, however, we made it without butter and well, you can guess how that went. There's no concept of leaving leftovers with Mac and cheese (even if it has no butter). You either finish it or someone finishes it for you (you might as well stuff yourself and have a great nap than give it up, no?).
Recipe
  • Boil the pasta in the water
  • Drain water once its done
  • Take a pan and put it on low heat
  • Add butter or margarine
  • Add milk
  • Add the Mac and cheese powder
  • Stir on low heat until creamy
Pro tips
  • Add more butter or milk for extra creaminess
  • Eat directly from the pan for max flavour
  • Add an egg if you're feeling ambitious
  • Add herbs on top if you want more flavour
Mistakes you're likely to make
  • Add salt
  • Forgetting you don't have butter in the fridge
  • The mix expires while you wait for the perfect time
